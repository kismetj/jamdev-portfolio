The Process

The path to the site or project you desire is a process that takes a few steps.
I want to ensure you have the project you're envisioning and you're happy with the final product.

Consult

Let's talk about your what your vision,needs,and objectives are. We'll discuss what you need and how I can help bring your solutions to life.

Proposal

Let's make it offical. I will provide a detailed proposal, that outlines the process, ins & outs, expected delivery and a contract to seal the deal.

Creation

The creation of your site begins. The building of your app or website is created according to the specifications chosen in the Consulting phase.

We will stay in contact with consistent updates, while I build your project.

Delivery

Once the project is done. We will give it a final look through and when satisfied, launch your site or app to the world.

Support

Once youre site launches successfully, you'll still need a hand from time to time. I offer a variety of support services from edits to updates, and ongoing support.

Communication

Throughout the process just as in life, Communication is key. Your vision is possible. Let's get your ideas from thought to creation and digitally out into the world!
